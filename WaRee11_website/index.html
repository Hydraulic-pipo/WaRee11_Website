Why WaRee11

Traditional river modeling is powerful, but often desktop-bound and difficult to teach.

WaRee11 brings a more visual, portable, and education-friendly workflow to iPadOS, making hydraulic modeling easier to explain, review, and iterate across classrooms, engineering teams, and research environments.

Problem

Desktop tools can slow down exploration

Traditional workflows often separate model setup, geometry editing, and results review into systems that are efficient for experts but harder for students, collaborators, and mobile-first teams to approach quickly.

Mission

A cleaner hydraulic workflow for learning and field-ready review

WaRee11 is shaped around touch-friendly modeling, clear visual hierarchy, and an intuitive sequence from project creation to hydraulic interpretation on iPad.
