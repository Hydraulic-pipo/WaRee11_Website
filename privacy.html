Privacy Policy

WaRee11 is being designed as a modern hydraulic modeling platform for iPadOS. This page explains how privacy is approached for the website and for user-created modeling work.

Current approach

  • WaRee11 currently does not require user accounts.
  • WaRee11 does not intentionally collect personal information.
  • User-created hydraulic model data is intended to remain under the user's control.

01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes the current privacy expectations for the WaRee11 website and the WaRee11 product concept. WaRee11 currently does not require user accounts and does not intentionally collect personal information through normal use of the website.

02

Information We Collect

At this stage, the website is primarily informational. WaRee11 currently does not require user accounts, and we do not intentionally request or collect personal information unless you choose to contact us directly by email or through a future clearly labeled form.

If a visitor sends an email, the information included in that message may be used only to respond to the inquiry or continue the requested conversation.

03

User-Created Modeling Data

User-created hydraulic model data is intended to remain under the user's control. Based on the current product direction, model files, geometry inputs, scenario setup, and results are not uploaded to an external server unless a future version clearly states otherwise.

If WaRee11 later introduces cloud sync, collaboration hosting, or remote processing, those features should be described explicitly with updated privacy information before they become part of standard use.

04

Website Analytics

The website may use basic analytics or hosting logs to understand page visits, performance, and general traffic patterns. These kinds of analytics are intended to help improve the website experience rather than identify individual visitors.

If analytics tooling becomes more advanced in the future, that change should be reflected in an updated version of this policy.

05

Third-Party Services

WaRee11 may rely on third-party services for website hosting, font delivery, or communication channels. Those providers may process limited technical information such as browser metadata, IP address, or delivery logs as part of operating their services.

Any third-party service used in a future product release should be introduced clearly and evaluated for alignment with the product's privacy expectations.

06

Children's Privacy

WaRee11 is intended for engineers, researchers, educators, and students working in hydraulic modeling contexts. The website is not designed to intentionally collect personal information from children.

If you believe personal information from a child has been submitted unintentionally, please contact us so that the request can be reviewed and addressed.

07

Data Security

Reasonable efforts are intended to be used to protect the website and any limited communications sent to the WaRee11 team. However, no website or communication method can guarantee absolute security.

As the product evolves, security practices may also evolve to match any new storage, synchronization, or collaboration capabilities.

08

Future Changes

WaRee11 is an evolving product. If privacy-related features, data handling, analytics, or hosted services change in the future, this Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect the new behavior.

Continued use of the website after a published update may indicate acceptance of the revised policy.

09

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or the current data handling approach for WaRee11 can be sent to contact@waree11.com.
